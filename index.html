About

I am a student at Bob Jones University studying Business in hopes to better prepare myself for working in supply chain management. I have been working in supply chain for the past five years managing inventory and deliveries for Chick-fil-A Frederick North. I am currently looking fro work in supply chain and construction management.

In my free time I hunt for meat and deer hides. I am working on tanning hides to sell as deer skin rugs.
